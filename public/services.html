Parents can also add notes or reminders related to feeding, such as dietary preferences or reactions to certain foods, ensuring a comprehensive overview of your child's nutritional intake.
Sleep Schedule Management: Our platform allows you to monitor nap times, nighttime sleep durations, and any sleep-related observations or routines. Whether you're establishing a sleep schedule or tracking changes in your child's sleep patterns over time, Cuddle Cloud provides the tools you need to promote healthy sleep habits.
Medical History Tracking: Keep your child's health information organized and accessible with Cuddle Cloud's medical history tracking feature. From vaccinations and illnesses to allergies and medications, you can maintain a comprehensive record of your child's health status and appointments with healthcare providers.
Multimedia Resources: Capture and cherish every moment with Cuddle Cloud's multimedia storage feature. From adorable baby photos to heartwarming video recordings, our platform lets you organize and preserve precious memories in one centralized location. With unlimited storage capacity, you can create a digital archive of your child's growth that can be revisited and shared with loved ones for years to come.

Share updates, photos and milestones with loved ones in real-time, regardless of distance with a seamless integration with popular social media platforms and a news feed, you can share updates with a wider audience while maintaining control over privacy settings.
